About Us

Welcome to Plagued by Poultry, where passion for gaming takes flight! We are a dynamic indie gaming company driven by a shared love for creating immersive and unforgettable gaming experiences.

Founded by a group of avid gamers, Plagued by Poultry was born out of a desire to bring unique, quirky, and captivating games to life. Our team of dedicated developers, artists, and storytellers believes that gaming should be an art form, a source of entertainment, and a way to connect with others.

What We Do

At Plagued by Poultry, we take pride in our commitment to creativity, innovation, and a touch of whimsy. Our games are a reflection of our colorful imaginations and a testament to our dedication to crafting enjoyable and engaging gameplay.

Who We Are

Join us on this extraordinary journey as we continue to hatch exciting new gaming experiences that will leave you both entertained and enthralled. Plagued by Poultry is not just a gaming company; it's a community of gamers, dreamers, and creators.
